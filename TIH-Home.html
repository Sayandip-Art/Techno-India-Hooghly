Vision & Mission

  • To provide pre-eminent educational experiences which are stimulating, responsive to needs of the century, and prepare our students for leadership in their profession and society.
  • To develop technical manpower by arranging short term courses, workshops, seminar on regular basis.
  • To create world class facilities and ambience for advance level of teaching and practical training for attracting best students from the country and abroad greater emphasis on research and collaborative works with industries and other institutes.
  • To improve efficiency and effectiveness of Institutional management of our Institute.
  • To train human resource in the era of cutting edge Technology.
  • To provide state - of- the - art technologies to prepare the students to man the technological frontiers of tomorrow.
  • To take active part in socio-economic development particularly of its region and the country as a whole.

Specialities & Achievements

  • Renowned college of West Bengal specialized for Computer Application and Management
  • Highly ranked with more than 90% Placement Record
  • University Rank Holders from Department of BBA in successive 3 years
  • University Rank Holders from Department of BCA & M.Sc.(CS) also
  • Library with huge collection of text and reference books and latest national and international journals and publications
  • Highly qualified and experienced teaching Professionals
  • Latest teaching methodology with latest technology
  • One and only college with 15:1 Student-Teacher ratio
