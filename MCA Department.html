Department of MCA


The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program is a two year full-time regular post gradguate course in Computer Application offered at our other campus Techno College Hooghly which is designed to produce a dynamic breed of computer professionals possessing excellent managerial skills. Emphasis is given to in-depth studies of numerous application-oriented subjects, covering various methodologies to develop software products efficiently and error-free, by utilizing advance networking technologies and application software.

Objectives :

  • Produce Postgraduates/Masters in Computer Science with Technical, Professional and Communication skills.
  • Train Future Industry Professionals.
After successful completion of MCA students will have:
  • A strong foundation of Computer Systems and Information Technology.
  • Dexterity in advanced programming languages.
  • The power to built sophisticated software for a wide range of applications.
  • The skills to work with higher end applications in Internet Technologies.
  • The managerial ability to analyse, design and develop software and to maintain software development.

Value Added Program :

  • Certification on latest technologies like Data Science, Web Development Using PHP, J2EE, UI Designing Using Angular JS etc.
  • Personality Improvement Program with Hands on Practice Tool.
  • Industrial Training for Minor & Major Project.
  • Live Projects and Industrial Visit Programs
  • Participation on Seminars and Workshops

Job & Future Prospects :

After successful completion of MCA you can work as:

  1. Software Programmer
  2. Database Administrator
  3. System and Network Administrator
  4. Multimedia Programmer.
  5. System Analyst
  6. Web Designer
  7. IT Manager
  8. Lecturer for Computer Science/Communication Technology.
  9. Further Study: If you want to enhance your IT skills you can further join PhD /M.Tech

Placement Assistance :

We have formed Placement cell to provide placement assistance to our students. Already 80% of pursuing students has been placed in various leading companies and organizations.

General Information

Duration Of The Course Two Years (Full-time course)
Medium of Instruction The medium of instruction is English
Eligibility

Passed BCA/ Bachelor Degree in Computer Science Engineering or equivalent Degree or Passed B.Sc. / B.Com. / B.A.with Mathematics at 10+2 Levelor at Graduation Level (with additional bridge Courses as per the norms of the concerned University). Obtained at least 50% marks (45% marks in case of candidates belonging to reserved category) in the qualifying Examination. Every Candidate must have a valid JECA* Rank or as per Government Rule.

* JECA is an Entrance Test Organized by West Bengal Joint Entrance Board.

Note:- Indian Nationals are only eligible to apply for this test.

For More Information about JECA visit: www.wbjeeb.in
